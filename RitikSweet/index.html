🏺 Our Legacy

Crafting Memories,
One Sweet at a Time.

For nearly four decades, Bhoop Singh Sweet House has been a cornerstone of local heritage. Every batch of our mithai is handcrafted using heirloom recipes passed through generations, ensuring that every bite tastes exactly like the nostalgia you cherish.

We use only pure ingredients — no preservatives, no artificial colours — because tradition demands nothing less.
